Privacy First

Your health data stays yours

We built Obsidian Health with a fundamental belief: your health data is deeply personal. That's why everything happens on-device. No cloud sync, no analytics, no compromise.

  • All processing happens locally on your iPhone
  • No account required — ever
  • No network requests to external servers
  • Data stored only in your chosen vault location
  • Open source core for full transparency
